Empowering Stories, Igniting Connections

Located in Dili, Bauang, La Union, DAScreatives Publishing is the home of ISEM Ilocandia – the lifestyle and leisure magazine of Northern Luzon that will make you smile.

We empower stories, ignite connections, and celebrate the essence of Northern Luzon through publications and events. With passion for creativity and commitment to excellence, we deliver publishing and event services that leave a lasting impact.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To craft compelling content, create unforgettable experiences, and build lasting relationships through publications and events, while promoting the best of Ilocandia.

Our Vision

To be the premier publishing and events company in Northern Luzon, known for showcasing the region's culture, lifestyle, and inspiring stories.

Our Gallery

Discover the vibrant world of ISEM Ilocandia through our curated gallery. From stunning landscapes and cultural heritage spots in Northern Luzon to behind-the-scenes glimpses of our publications and events, each image tells a story of creativity, passion, and connection.
